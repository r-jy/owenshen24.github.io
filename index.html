mindlevelup

mindlevelup is a blog I write on human rationality, a look at how humans make decisions and how we might be able to make better ones. New essays come out almost every week, ranging from somewhat well-researched to purely speculative. Topics cover habits, planning, motivation, and introspection.

For a more structured overview, I’ve also written a book that’s adapted much of its content from the blog posts. That’s freely available here.
